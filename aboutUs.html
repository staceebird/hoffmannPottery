About Me


Colter Hoffmann is a 21 year old artist based near Ogden, Ut. He has be working with ceramics since the age of 14 and has participated in many art shows and competitions across northern utah, and has also been featured in many galleries since that time. Colter gets inspiration for his work from Greek and Asian styles of pottery, and also incorporates modern art styles into his own unique designs which are revered by many.
